Carolyn Seiler

Florida-based visual artist & creative studio founder

Originally from Long Island, New York, Carolyn studied art at the University of New Hampshire and received a BFA in painting. She lives with her Search and Rescue husband (a real life hero, now retired) and their occasional cat.

Twenty years in Key West shaped this artist's colorful whimsical style. Carolyn paints and draws the world around her — her cats, the hurricanes, or the changing light on the Indian River are all sources of inspiration. She now runs an artist co‑op “Carolyn Seiler & Friends” in Cocoa Village FL. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}
